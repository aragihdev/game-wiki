Sans

The Sentry of Snowdin and Judge of the Underground

Sans is the brother of Papyrus and a major supporting character in Undertale. He first appears in the forest of Snowdin, acting as both a comedic foil and a watchful presence throughout the protagonist's journey.

Physical Characteristics: A short, stout skeleton with a permanent grin. He wears a blue unzipped hoodie, a white t-shirt, black shorts with white stripes, and pink slippers. His left eye can glow blue and yellow during intense combat.

Personality

Sans is famously laid-back and lazy, often seen sleeping on the job or taking multiple breaks at his many sentry stations:

  • Comedian: He is a lover of puns and practical jokes, frequently using his "whoopee cushion in the hand" trick or selling "hot cats" to the protagonist.
  • Observant: Beneath his easygoing exterior, Sans is incredibly sharp. He often breaks the fourth wall and shows an awareness of the game's mechanics, such as EXP, LOVE, and SAVEs.
  • Nihilism: Due to his knowledge of timeline resets, Sans suffers from a lack of motivation, feeling that any progress made will eventually be erased.

Abilities and Powers

Despite having only "1 ATK and 1 DEF," Sans is widely considered the most difficult opponent in the game due to his mastery of space and time:

  • Gaster Blasters: He summons skull-like devices that fire powerful beams of light.
  • Blue Magic: He can manipulate the protagonist's soul gravity, forcing it to fall or slam against walls, and can teleport himself or others instantly (his "shortcuts").
  • Karmic Retribution (KR): His attacks inflict a poison-like effect that drains health over time, punishing those who have committed many sins.
  • Evasion: He is the only character in the game who can actively dodge the protagonist's attacks.

History

The origins of Sans and his brother are shrouded in mystery, as they "appeared one day and asserted themselves" in Snowdin.

  • The Judge: Sans serves as the final judge of the protagonist's actions. At the end of the journey, he evaluates the player's "Level of Violence" in the Last Corridor.
  • The Promise: He reveals that he only protected the human because of a promise he made to Toriel behind the door of the Ruins, stating that without it, the protagonist would have been "dead where they stand."

Trivia

  • Font Name: Like his brother, his name and dialogue are based on a font; in his case, "Comic Sans."
  • The Grillby's Regular: He is a frequent customer at Grillby's, where he has a massive tab that he never pays.
  • Scientific Background: His hidden workshop and his dialogue suggest he may have a background in physics or a connection to the former Royal Scientist, W.D. Gaster.
