Asgore Dreemurr

The King of the Monsters and Lord of the Underground

Asgore is the ruler of the Underground and the final obstacle for any human wishing to leave. He is a tragic figure who serves as the penultimate boss in many routes, forced by duty to collect seven human souls to shatter the barrier and free his people.

Physical Characteristics: A large, broad-shouldered Boss Monster with golden fur and a thick beard. He wears massive golden armor, a purple cape with the Delta Rune, and a small crown. In battle, he wields a giant red trident.

Personality

Despite his intimidating stature and the dark task he must perform, Asgore is a gentle and kind-hearted soul:

  • Reluctant Warrior: He does not truly wish to kill anyone. He often offers the protagonist tea and asks them if they are "ready" before fighting, clearly stalling for time and hoping for another way.
  • Fatherly Nature: He has a deep love for gardening (specifically golden flowers) and simple pleasures. He is frequently described by other monsters as a "big fuzzy pushover."
  • Remorseful: Asgore is haunted by his past decisions and the loss of his family, carrying the crushing guilt of the war he declared against humanity.

Abilities and Powers

Asgore is one of the most powerful beings in the world, possessing immense magical and physical strength:

  • Fire Manipulation: Like Toriel, he can summon massive waves of fireballs, but his patterns are faster and more complex.
  • Trident Mastery: He uses his trident to create sweeping orange and blue attacks, forcing the protagonist to either stay moving or remain perfectly still.
  • Destruction of Mercy: At the start of his battle, Asgore literally destroys the "MERCY" button, showing his belief that a peaceful resolution is no longer possible for him.

History

Asgore was once a happy father and husband, living in peace with Toriel and their son, Asriel.

  • The Declaration: After the tragic death of Asriel and the first fallen human (Chara) in a single night, Asgore was consumed by grief and declared that any human who fell into the Underground must die.
  • The Separation: His declaration led to Toriel leaving him in disgust, leaving Asgore to rule alone in "New Home," waiting for the final soul needed to end the exile of his kind.

Trivia

  • Bad at Naming: It is a recurring joke that Asgore is terrible at naming things; "Home," "New Home," and even his son "Asriel" (a combination of Asgore and Toriel) are prime examples.
  • Santa Claus Connection: During the holidays, Asgore often dresses up as Santa and gives gifts to the children of the Underground.
  • Favorite Tea: His favorite drink is Golden Flower Tea, which he often shares with visitors to his throne room.
