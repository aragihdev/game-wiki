Alphys

The Royal Scientist and Engineer of the Core

Alphys is the brilliant but socially anxious Royal Scientist of the Underground. She first encounters the protagonist in her lab in Hotland, where she attempts to guide them through the region's many traps while secretly struggling with the weight of her past mistakes.

Physical Characteristics: A reptilian, yellow-skinned monster who wears a white lab coat and round spectacles. She is slightly hunched and often seen sweating or stuttering when nervous.

Personality

Alphys is an incredibly intelligent individual who suffers from deep-seated insecurity and guilt:

  • Nerd Culture: She is a massive fan of human media, specifically anime (her favorite being "Mew Mew Kissy Cutie") and manga. She often shares her theories and fanfiction on social media.
  • Social Anxiety: Alphys finds it difficult to communicate with others and often relies on her phone or computer to interact. She is deeply in love with Undyne but is terrified of being rejected.
  • Internal Conflict: Beneath her cheerful "guide" persona, Alphys carries a heavy burden regarding her secret experiments with Determination, which she believes make her a failure.

Abilities and Powers

While Alphys rarely engages in direct combat, her genius-level intellect makes her one of the most influential characters in the Underground:

  • Technological Mastery: She built Mettaton and maintains the complex machinery of the CORE, the source of all power in the Underground.
  • Hacking and Surveillance: She monitors the protagonist's entire journey through cameras hidden throughout the world and can hack into systems to open doors or disable traps.
  • Chemistry and Biology: As the Royal Scientist, she possesses vast knowledge of monster souls, alchemy, and the extraction of Determination.

History

Alphys was appointed as the Royal Scientist by King Asgore after she claimed to have created a soul (which was actually Mettaton).

  • The Amalgamates: In an attempt to find a way to break the barrier, Alphys experimented on "fallen" monsters by injecting them with Determination. The results were horrific, leading to the creation of the Amalgamates, whom she kept hidden in the True Lab.
  • Redemption: In the True Pacifist Route, the protagonist helps Alphys face her past, leading her to confess her secrets and finally find the confidence to be honest with herself and her friends.

Trivia

  • Instant Noodles: Alphys has a notorious obsession with instant noodles, which are the only thing she seems to know how to cook (or eat).
  • The Mew Mew Debate: She has a strong, polarizing opinion on "Mew Mew Kissy Cutie 2," which she considers to be an absolute disgrace compared to the original.
  • Self-Insert: She often uses the protagonist as a way to "live out" her favorite anime tropes, such as the hero being guided by a mysterious voice.
