Mettaton

The Killer Robot and Underground's Only Star

Mettaton is a soul-driven robot designed by Alphys. Originally built as a human-hunting machine, he pivoted to show business, becoming the premier entertainer of the Underground and the host of every television show on the MTT network.

Physical Characteristics: In his initial form, he is a gray, rectangular box with a grid of lights for a face and a single wheel. In his "EX" form, he becomes a sleek, humanoid robot with pink accents, long black hair, and incredibly stylish legs.

Personality

Mettaton is a charismatic, narcissistic, and incredibly dramatic performer who lives for the spotlight:

  • Showman: He treats every encounter like a television segment, ranging from cooking shows to news broadcasts and deadly quiz games.
  • Ambition: His ultimate dream is to reach the surface, not just for freedom, but to perform for a human audience of millions and become a global superstar.
  • Hidden Care: Despite his ego, he deeply values his fans and, in the Pacifist Route, realizes that the monsters of the Underground need him as a beacon of hope and entertainment.

Abilities and Powers

Mettaton's combat style is a mix of high-tech weaponry and theatrical flair, designed to keep the "Ratings" high:

  • Mini-Mettatons: He can deploy tiny versions of himself that throw hearts or umbrellas at the protagonist.
  • Heart-to-Heart Combat: In his EX form, his "heart" (his core) is vulnerable, but he uses it to fire intense bursts of energy and electric bolts.
  • Yellow Magic: During his battles, the protagonist's soul is often turned upside down (yellow), allowing them to fire bullets to destroy Mettaton's obstacles.
  • Mettaton NEO: In the Genocide Route, he transforms into a high-defense combat model, though he remains famously fragile.

History

Before becoming a robot, Mettaton was a ghost (the cousin of Napstablook) who dreamed of having a physical body to perform in.

  • The Arrangement: He struck a deal with Alphys, who promised to build him a body if he helped her impress King Asgore by acting as a "rogue" robot for the scientist to "defeat."
  • The Star is Born: Once he inhabited his new body, he quickly rose to fame, creating a massive brand that includes a hotel, a resort, and countless merchandise items throughout Hotland.

Trivia

  • The Mystery Key: The player can buy a key from Bratty and Catty that opens the "house next to Napstablook's," revealing diaries about Mettaton's life as a ghost.
  • Leg Obsession: Mettaton is incredibly proud of his legs, which are frequently mentioned in his dialogue and are a central part of his "EX" reveal.
  • Ratings System: His boss fight is unique because the player can win by simply entertaining the audience and reaching a certain number of "Ratings" rather than fighting.
