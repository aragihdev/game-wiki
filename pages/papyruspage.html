Papyrus

The Aspiring Royal Guard and Master of Spaghetti

Papyrus is the flamboyant and energetic younger brother of Sans. He is a major character who first appears in the woods of Snowdin, where he spends his time dreaming of joining the Royal Guard and capturing a human to gain popularity and respect.

Physical Characteristics: A tall, lanky skeleton with a distinctive "battle body" outfit. He wears a white chestplate with gold trim, a tattered red scarf (or cape), blue briefs with a golden belt, and bright red boots and gloves. His face is highly expressive, often featuring wide eyes and a toothy grin.

Personality

Papyrus is defined by his boundless optimism, loud confidence, and surprisingly kind heart:

  • Ambitious: He is obsessed with status and "popularity," believing that capturing a human will finally earn him the friends and admiration he deserves.
  • Innocence: Despite his "battle body" and attempts to be intimidating, Papyrus is incredibly innocent and often lacks common sense. He is one of the few characters who genuinely believes everyone can be a good person if they try.
  • Ego (with a Heart): He speaks in capital letters and often refers to himself as "THE GREAT PAPYRUS," yet he is the first to offer help or a "date" to the protagonist.

Abilities and Powers

Papyrus is a skilled combatant who specializes in bone-based magic, though he is careful never to actually kill his opponent:

  • Bone Attacks: He summons rows of white bones of varying heights. Like his brother, he can also use "Blue Magic" to turn the protagonist's soul blue, forcing it to obey gravity and jump over obstacles.
  • Non-Lethal Intent: Papyrus is the only boss in the game who will stop attacking if the protagonist's health is too low (1 HP), capturing them and putting them in his "shed" instead of killing them.
  • Master of Puzzles: He prides himself on his elaborate (though often flawed) puzzles, such as the "Electric Maze" and the "Tile Puzzle," designed to test the human's wits.

History

Like Sans, Papyrus's past is largely unknown before his arrival in Snowdin.

  • The Royal Guard Trainee: He is currently under the "tutelage" of Undyne. Although Undyne knows he is too kind for the Royal Guard, she gives him "cooking lessons" (specifically spaghetti) to keep him occupied and happy.
  • The Bridge to Friendship: In the Pacifist Route, Papyrus becomes the protagonist's biggest supporter, eventually acting as a mediator between the human and the other monsters.

Trivia

  • Font Name: His name and dialogue font are based on the "Papyrus" typeface, usually rendered in all caps.
  • Culinary Skills: Despite his passion for spaghetti, the game implies that he has never actually eaten it himself, and his cooking is described as having an "indescribable" (often metallic) taste.
  • Social Media: He is very active on the Underground's social network, often posting updates about his puzzles and his "coolness."
