Undyne

The Captain of the Royal Guard and Hero of the Underground

Undyne is the fierce and hot-headed leader of the Royal Guard. She first appears as a mysterious armored figure pursuing the protagonist through Waterfall, driven by her duty to collect the final human soul needed to shatter the barrier.

Physical Characteristics: An anthropomorphic blue fish-like monster with red hair tied in a ponytail. She wears an eye patch over her left eye and is typically seen in full gray plate armor. In her casual attire, she wears a black tank top and jeans.

Personality

Undyne is characterized by her intense energy, passion, and unwavering loyalty to monsterkind:

  • Heroic Spirit: She views herself as the protector of the Underground and will do anything to free her people, even if it means being ruthless toward humans.
  • Hot-Tempered: Everything Undyne does is "to the extreme," whether it's cooking, training, or fighting. She is prone to shouting and breaking things in her excitement.
  • Deep Compassion: Despite her aggression, she cares deeply for her friends, especially Papyrus (whom she mentors) and Alphys (for whom she harbors romantic feelings).

Abilities and Powers

Undyne is one of the physically strongest monsters in the game, capable of lifting boulders and shattering stone with her bare hands:

  • Energy Spears: She can summon glowing blue spears of light to throw at the protagonist or summon them from the ground.
  • Green Magic: She can turn the protagonist's soul green, which prevents them from moving but allows them to block incoming spears with a shield.
  • Undyne the Undying: In the Genocide Route, her pure will to protect the world allows her to refuse death, transforming into a much more powerful form fueled by natural determination.

History

Undyne was a rowdy child who once challenged King Asgore to a fight. Impressed by her spirit, he trained her himself.

  • Rise to Captain: Through her strength and charisma, she climbed the ranks to become the head of the Royal Guard, inspiring monsters everywhere with her bravery.
  • The Lesson: After the protagonist shows her mercy (or saves her from heatstroke), she can become a close ally, eventually realizing that not all humans are the monsters she was taught to hate.

Trivia

  • Anime Enthusiast: Thanks to Alphys, Undyne is a huge fan of human anime, though she mistakenly believes it is a factual historical record of human life.
  • Cooking Disaster: Her "cooking lessons" usually result in her house catching on fire because she believes that "stirring" should be done with a spear.
  • Piano Skills: Despite her rough exterior, she is an accomplished piano player, which can be heard in the background music of her home.
